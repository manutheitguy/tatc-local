What We Export

Here's a list of our major imports to give a rough idea

Kenyan pineapples usually do well in areas where the climate is sufficiently humid, warm, and free from extreme temperatures. This right combination of heat and moisture is one of the most important factors to consider in order to get the best quality of pineapples. It is usually grown for its fruit and eaten fresh in salads, desserts, processed in juice, jam, dried fruits; in pastries, pudding and in sauces. They can be exported whole or canned

Kenya is universally admired to have the finest Arabica coffee in the world despite its proximity to Ethiopia which is believed to have been the region where coffee originated. Kenya is the most balanced and most complex of all coffee origins. Kenyan coffee is well known for its intense flavor, full body with floral tastes and deep aromatic overtones making it one of of the most sought-after coffee in the world. It is used mainly in blending and specialty.

Kenyan Tea has won international accolades due to its superior, distinctive and spectacular quality, characteristics, taste and aroma. The quality of black tea depends on the number of top young leaves harvested, the mode of harvesting and the care with which the green leaves are handled and skillfully processed.
